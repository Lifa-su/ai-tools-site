## What is AI Image Generation?

AI image generation uses machine learning models to create images from text descriptions (called "prompts"). You type what you want to see, and the AI creates it — from photorealistic photos to abstract art, product mockups to fantasy landscapes.

In 2025, the technology has matured to the point where AI-generated images are used in professional marketing, publishing, and design workflows every day.

## How Does It Work?

Most AI image generators use a technique called **diffusion**. The model starts with random noise and gradually refines it into a coherent image, guided by your text prompt. Think of it like a sculptor chipping away at marble — except the sculptor works in milliseconds.

The key models powering today's tools:
- **Midjourney V6** — Known for artistic quality and aesthetics
- **DALL-E 3** — OpenAI's model, excellent at following complex prompts
- **Stable Diffusion 3** — Open-source, highly customizable
- **Flux** — The rising star with impressive photorealism

**Price:** Free (self-hosted) / Various cloud options
**Best for:** Technical users, custom workflows, privacy-conscious creators

## Prompt Writing Tips

The quality of your output depends heavily on your prompt. Here are proven techniques:

### 1. Be Specific
Bad: "a cat"
Good: "a fluffy orange tabby cat sitting on a windowsill, golden hour sunlight, shallow depth of field, shot on 35mm film"

### 2. Describe the Style
Include artistic direction: "watercolor painting," "cinematic lighting," "minimalist flat design," "photorealistic 8K"

### 3. Specify What You Do NOT Want
Most tools support negative prompts. Use them to exclude unwanted elements: "no text, no watermark, no blurry"

### 4. Use Reference Artists or Styles
"In the style of Studio Ghibli" or "Wes Anderson color palette" can dramatically shape the output.

### 5. Iterate
Your first generation is rarely your best. Use variations, re-rolls, and refined prompts to get closer to your vision.
