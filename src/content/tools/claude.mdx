## What is Claude?

Claude is an AI assistant built by Anthropic, a safety-focused AI company founded by former OpenAI researchers. Named after Claude Shannon, the father of information theory, Claude has quickly become one of the top alternatives to ChatGPT.

What sets Claude apart is its emphasis on helpfulness, harmlessness, and honesty. In practice, this means Claude tends to produce more thoughtful, nuanced responses — especially for complex topics.

## Key Features

- **200K Token Context Window**: Process entire books, codebases, or lengthy documents in a single conversation
- **Artifacts**: Create and iterate on documents, code, and interactive content in a side panel
- **Projects**: Organize conversations with custom instructions and knowledge bases
- **Vision**: Analyze images, charts, screenshots, and documents
- **API Access**: Build applications with Claude's capabilities
- **Team Plans**: Collaborate with shared projects and conversations

## Who Is Claude Best For?

Claude is ideal for **writers, researchers, and developers** who work with long documents. The 200K context window is a game-changer for anyone who needs to analyze lengthy content. It also excels at coding tasks, particularly code review and refactoring.

If you value thoughtful, well-reasoned responses over speed, Claude is your best bet.

## The Verdict

Claude has carved out a strong niche as the "thinking person's AI." While ChatGPT may be more versatile with its plugin ecosystem, Claude consistently produces higher-quality long-form content and more nuanced analysis.

**Rating: 4.6/5** — The best choice for long-form writing and deep analysis.
