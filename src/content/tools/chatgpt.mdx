## What is ChatGPT?

ChatGPT is OpenAI's flagship AI chatbot, launched in November 2022. It quickly became the fastest-growing consumer app in history, reaching 100 million users in just two months.

Built on the GPT-4 architecture, ChatGPT can handle an impressive range of tasks: writing essays, debugging code, analyzing data, creating content, answering questions, and much more.

## Key Features

- **GPT-4 & GPT-4o**: Access to OpenAI's most capable models
- **Code Interpreter**: Run Python code, analyze files, create charts
- **DALL-E Integration**: Generate images directly in chat
- **GPT Store**: Access thousands of custom GPTs for specific tasks
- **Web Browsing**: Search the internet for up-to-date information
- **Voice Mode**: Have spoken conversations with the AI
- **File Upload**: Analyze documents, images, and spreadsheets

## Who Is ChatGPT Best For?

ChatGPT is ideal for **anyone who works with text** — writers, developers, students, marketers, and researchers. The free tier is surprisingly capable, making it accessible to everyone.

If you need a single AI tool that does a bit of everything, ChatGPT is the safest bet.

## The Verdict

ChatGPT remains the gold standard for AI chatbots. While competitors like Claude and Gemini are catching up in specific areas, ChatGPT's combination of versatility, ecosystem, and constant improvement keeps it at the top.

**Rating: 4.7/5** — The best all-around AI tool for most users.
